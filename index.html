The Story

Deady Casket is a Gothic, Afro‑Punk, Hip‑Hop, Trapmetal, and Horrorcore artist whose sound and presence defy easy categorization. Born in Milwaukee, raised in Minneapolis, and now rooted in Denver, he’s carved a path across the United States—meeting fans, commanding stages, acting on screen, and leaving a mark in every medium he touches. Music is the foundation, but Deady’s creativity runs wider: he’s landed film roles and built IMFD Streetwear, his award‑winning fashion brand. A true multi‑hyphenate, he expands his spectrum with each project—pushing boundaries, elevating culture, and passing his craft forward.
